This is a simple and interesting bomb blasting game played against computer.
The basic idea of the game is that the player and computer place 5 different
kinds of bomb in their grids of size 10 X 10 boxes.  Then the player and 
computer blasts opposite party’s bombs by revealing one box at a time
simultaneously. Who blasts all bombs of opposite party first he wins the game.
